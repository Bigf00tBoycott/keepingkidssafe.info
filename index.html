Keeping Kids Safe in OKC

Lead Pipes Are Poisoning Our Future. It's Time for Action.

Why Lead Pipes Are a Threat

Lead exposure from old pipes in Oklahoma City is a silent but deadly threat. Children are especially vulnerable—lead damages brain development, learning ability, and overall health. There's no safe level of lead. We must remove every single lead pipe in our city.

What's Happening in OKC?

Despite federal funding opportunities and the science being clear, OKC has been slow to act. This delay puts thousands of children at risk. Our city can and must do better.

Demand action. Email the Mayor or contact your council rep now.

We’re not asking for miracles. We’re demanding accountability. Replacing lead pipes is the bare minimum. Join us in protecting Oklahoma City's kids.
